Top most Tea Gardens in Munnar

Kolukkumalai Tea Estate

Located about 35 kms from Munnar, this stunning tea garden is one of the most beautiful tea estates in India, and is rated among the top places for tea production.

Pallivasal Tea Garden

While there are lakes, and waterfalls in Munnar, visiting the tea estate has its own unique charm. The vast rolling hills brimmed with greenery and the mellow aroma of the tea fills your heart with unimaginable joy.

Lockhart Tea Estate

Lockhart Estate is one of the oldest tea gardens in Munnar. It was established by John Von Rosenberg, and his son.
